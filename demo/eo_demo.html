Epistemic Observability (EO) brings automatic context tracking, superposition of multiple values, and stability classification to your data. This demo features the Three-Level Detail System:

  • Level 1 (Inline): Hover over cells to see type badges and indicators. Double-click to edit.
  • Level 2 (Record Modal): Click the first cell in any row to view the full record with tabs for Fields, Relationships, Provenance, History, and Context.
  • Level 3 (Field Lens): In the record modal, click any field label to open the deep field inspector showing definitions, provenance, relationships, data lineage, and actions.
